The Wow! Momo Story

Started by classmates Sagar Daryani and Binod Homagai,  Kolkata based company Wow! Momo was started on 29th August 2008. Hailing from St Xavier’s Kolkata in their final year of their graduation they took the final plunge and decided to give consumers an experience and not just Momo! So simply they called it Wow! Momo.  The basic idea was to create an experience that would leave their customers with only one word and that is WOW!

If you have grown up in Kolkata – you ought to be a foodie and if you are a foodie you would know the fellow foodie’s centricity and preference.

Some of our wide range of products with which have experimented include Cheese Momos, Augratin,  Sizzlers,  Butter Masala Momo, Darjeeling Momo. The Moburg was a big innovation that rightly combined Momos with Burgers and was a lip-smacking meal for everyone. The journey from just playing with Momos to introducing so many new dishes has been challenging and excruciating but a roller coaster ride.

Today Wow! Momo has over 300 outlets across 17 cities and the hunger and passion for what they do still lingers!

Redefining Chindian

After successfully building the brand Wow! Momo the brand has launched its second brand Wow! China. Wow! China is an initiative by Wow! Momo to redefine the Chindian cuisine across the country.  The Chindian cuisine is the Indian version of  Chinese which is popular all over the country.

Treat to the taste buds!

Making food fun has always been our motto and innovation has always kept us ahead.  Some of our popular dishes include Khao Suey,  Spring Rolls,  Honey Potato,  Chicken Wrapped Prawns,  Chinese Bhel,  Peppery Lemon Paneer . The brand serves some exclusive rice bowls which is something that stands out on the menu.
